The Project

The Free Mind Project aim to publish RPGs, games and comics on topics like Philosophy, Literature, History, Social Sciences, etc. And in multiple languages (Portuguese and English, for now). Because it's much more easy to learn you have fun. And also because it's so cool to have fun with what you learn.

Our Mission is to provide easy access to the most important discussions of humanity. And have fun during the process.

The Author

My name is Almir Paulo: Master degree in Philosophy, self-taught programmer (and other things...), enthusiast for esoteric/mystic topics, chess player for hobby, RPG GM (when I can), Nerd, and freelance translator, ghostwriter and editor.

This project is the way I found to channel all my creativity ad skills to one point. I have a variety of interests in many different topics and I love to keep learning new skills.

The Audience

Maybe you are not exactly a teacher or a student, which certainly is the kind of people that get the most beneficial from our products, but you would likea different approach on your next RPG session, to play a new interesting indie game or to read a different comic that fairly maches your nerdness. We are here for you too.
